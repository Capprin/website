Selected Work

Wine Classification. I wrote a paper detailing a method to classify wine quality based on chemical properties. It was based on prior work, but my method was able to provide good classification by reducing the resolution of the "quality" parameter.

Procecdural Story Generation. An ongoing (ish) project of mine to produce believable and interesting stories from a collection of vignettes. The motivation is that procedurally generated games lack depth, a problem I'm trying to solve by creating narrative. The project takes advantage of neo4j to organize vignettes by inputs, actions, and outputs in a searchable way.

Spectral Analysis. For my undergraduate capstone, I wrote another paper with a group of peers, exploring signal processing and audio compression. The project expanded my understanding of the Fourier transform, in its many forms.

United States Geological Survey. For a year and a half, I worked at the Geologic Hazards Science Center. I contributed to their web architecture, both contributing to existing web services and implementing cloud services in AWS.
