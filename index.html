I'm a Robotics Master's student at Oregon State University, working in the Laboratory for Robotics and Applied Mechanics. I'm interested in the use of mathematics to robustly describe real-world behavior, and in effective approaches for visualization and communication of mathematics. These two interests provide stronger understanding of natural phenomena, and allow us to construct agents that interact with the natural world.

I graduated with a B.S. from Colorado School of Mines in Computational and Applied mathematics, minoring in Biomechanical engineering.
